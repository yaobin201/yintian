Schmidt ID-TECH Limited

Schmidt ID-TECH Limited is a premier Automatic Identification and Data Capture (AIDC) system integrator in Asia. We specialize in deploying a diverse range of technology platforms—including Barcode, RFID, Voice Recognition, Mobile Computing, Wireless Communications and Robotics—to streamline business processes and enhance everyday life. Our solutions are designed to drive operational efficiency and deliver practical benefits across industries.

COMPANY STRUCTURE

Schmidt ID-TECH Limited maintains a strong and strategic presence across the Asia Pacific region, with regional offices in Australia, China, Macau, Malaysia, Singapore, Taiwan, and Thailand.

Our head office is based in Hong Kong, one of the world’s leading international financial hubs. From here, we drive corporate strategy, ensure compliance, and provide operational support for all other regional offices.

We operate through a simple yet effective management structure, organized into three core areas that work in close synergy to deliver seamless, end-to-end solutions for our customers.

We specialize in innovative solutions of Barcoding, RFID and Mobile Computing. It distributes and integrates applications for many world-class vendors such as Zebra, Honeywell, Datalogic, Urovo and Geek+ in Asia. We also develop its own range of proprietary software for asset tracking and management, warehouse management, route sales management, mobile proof of delivery and software solutions for customized applications.

The Competence Centre is established to harness new technologies and integrate them into our existing platform. The Centre has a well-established team of highly skilled professionals working on RFID hardware and software development and recently has extended its expertise in voice-directed applications and Robotics businesses.

MILESTONES
2006

Schmidt ID-TECH Limited began operations in 2006 under the name ID-TECH (Hong Kong) Limited. The company initially focused on providing Auto-ID equipment and solutions to clients across various sectors in Hong Kong—including retail, logistics, construction, and government institutions. Starting with just five employees, the company rapidly expanded and quickly established a strong foothold in the Auto-ID industry, securing a significant share of the market.

2016

In 2016, Schmidt Electronics Group Limited acquired ID-TECH (Hong Kong) Limited to strengthen its data capture and information management business in the region. Following the acquisition, the company was rebranded as Schmidt ID-TECH Limited.

2023

In 2023, Schmidt ID-TECH Limited officially spun off to become an independent entity, solely focused on the Auto-ID business.

2025

Today, Schmidt ID-TECH Limited is more than a distributor or reseller—it is a technically-driven company with a strong engineering foundation and extensive capabilities. The company now delivers integrated IT solutions that enhance operational efficiency and improve everyday life across industries.

2025

With over 50 staff members located throughout Asia, Schmidt ID-TECH Limited continues to embrace innovation by staying at the forefront of technology. We are committed to delivering advanced solutions that respond to the evolving needs of the global market and provide exceptional value to our customers.
